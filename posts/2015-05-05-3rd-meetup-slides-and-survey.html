Next meetup

Next meetup is, as always, last Wednesday of the month, May 27th.
We’ll have two talks as usual:

“Introduction to Rust” by Nerijus Arlauskas (@nercury)

Rust 1.0 stable will be released on May 15th. To celebrate, Nerijus will give you a brief introduction to Rust, explaining its main features, why people call it “the C++ killer” and how, under the hood, it’s really a functional language.

“Monoids and friends” by Saulius Valatka (@saulius_vl)

Saulius Valatka will continue our journey of exploring algebraic structures in programming. This time, we hit the ubiquitous monoids. More info soon.
